Biography

Hang Yin is a Ph.D. candidate in College of Control Science & Engineering at Zhejiang University, under the supervision of Prof. Dibo Hou. His Ph.D. committee includes Prof. Pingjie Huang and Dr. Jie Yu, and he is also working closely with Prof. Guangxin Zhang and Dr. Yunqi Cao.

He earned his Master of Philosophy (M.Sc.) degree from the College of Control Science & Engineering at Zhejiang University(ZJU). Before that, he received his B.Eng degree from Sichuan University (SCU) in 2015, where he worked with Prof. Qun Zhou and Prof. Kai Liu.

His current research focuses on machine/deep learning algorithms and their applications in optical sensing technology with intelligent in-situ device.
