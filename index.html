Biography

Hang Yin is a Ph.D. candidate in College of Control Science & Engineering at Zhejiang University, under the supervision of Prof. Dibo Hou. His Ph.D. committee includes Prof. Pingjie Huang and Dr. Jie Yu, and he is also working closely with Prof. Guangxin Zhang and Dr. Yunqi Cao.

He earned his Master of Philosophy (M.PHIL) degree from the Control Science & Engineering at Zhejiang University. Before that, he received his B.Eng degree from Sichuan University (SCU) in 2015, where he worked with Prof. Qun Zhou and Prof. Kai Liu.

His current research focuses on machine/deep learning algorithms and their applications in water environment perception with intelligent in-situ device.

Key Projects

  • Sep. 2018–Present, Deep learning identification of organic compounds in water based on online spectral data

    • This is a Ph.D. research project. In view of the fact that spectral analysis theory of traditional chemical analysis method is highly professional and dependent on the experience and ability of related experts, which is difficult to implement analysis system, an automatic water quality monitoring scheme is proposed.

    • The core technologies involved as follows: Deep Learning (few-shot or zeros-shot)，Molecular fingerprint of organic substances, three-dimensional fluorescence spectroanalysis, absorption spectroanalysis

    • Mainly funded by the following projects:

      • The National Key Research and Development Program of China (No. 2017YFC1403801) Study on spectral detection method of TOC in seawater.

      • National Natural Science Foundation of China (No 61573313)Detecting and classifying anomaly water quality events based on multi-source information fusion

      • Key R&D project of Zhejiang Province(No. 2015C03014)Study on intelligent management and long-term operation mechanism of river regulation and maintenance
